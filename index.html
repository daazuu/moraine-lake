About

It has the most beautiful color, with shades of a green-blue that changes intensity as the summer goes on and the glaciers melt. It's in the Valley of the Ten Peaks, so it's surrounded by gorgeous mountains, waterfalls and rocks, making such an breath taking scene that feels unreal. The place has the most pure fresh air and allows you to explore by canoeing or hiking.
